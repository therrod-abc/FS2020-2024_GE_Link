               MSFS GEOGRAPHY FLIGHT SYNC - VERSION 1.0                
=======================================================================

    1. QUICK START GUIDE  All tracks are recorded and distance travel displayed in the HUD.  
	after each session you must delete or toggle off previous flights GE sidebar.  book keeping 
	
	First time run after unzipping.  must activate the created FS_Network_Master.kml to green light the sidebar. 
	
    ---------------------------------------------------------------
    * EXTRACT: Unzip this entire folder to your Desktop or a 
               dedicated flight folder.
    * LAUNCH:  Double-click "config.bat" to start. or edit config.ini manually
    * VIEW:    Open "Google Earth Pro" to see your aircraft.  Only after a flight is loaded.

    2. OPERATING MODES
    ---------------------------------------------------------------
    This program is a 3-in-1 utility that adapts to how you fly:

        [SOLO MODE] - (Stand-Alone Tracker)
        Choose "Guest" in the launcher and enter your own local IP
        (127.0.0.1)  The program will track 
        your flight privately for personal review.

        [NETWORK MODE] - (Collaborative )
        * HOST : Select "Host" to start the central server.
        * GUEST  Select "Guest" and enter the Host IP.
          This will "Push" your position to the host and "Pull" 
          the hosts position onto your map in real-time.

    3. GOOGLE EARTH SIDEBAR (What to look for)
    ---------------------------------------------------------------
    Check the "Temporary Places" folder in the left sidebar:

        > LIVE FLIGHT PATH: 
          The active 3D line showing exactly where you have flown. 
		  can be toggled on or off.  keeps recording either way

        > AIRCRAFT ICON: 
          A 3D model representing your live location and heading.

        > FLIGHT HUD: 
          On-screen text showing Airspeed, Altitude, and Heading. Can be toggled on or off

        > REFRESH LINK: 
          The "engine" fetching data every 0.5 seconds. Ensure this 
          remains checked to keep the map moving.

    4. TROUBLESHOOTING & SAFETY
    ---------------------------------------------------------------
    * WINDOWS WARNING: 
      If a blue "Windows Protected Your PC" box appears, click 
      "More Info" and then "Run Anyway." This is normal for 
      independent flight tools.

    * CONNECTION ERRORS: 
      Ensure you are actually in the cockpit of your aircraft in 
      MSFS before starting the program.

    * FIREWALLS: 
      If flying with others, ensure the "Host" has allowed Port 80 
      through their local Windows Firewall settings. or configure to 
	  another open port that has been forwarded.
